Referral System

Enhance your Adventure Mode experience with our Referral System! Here's how it works:

Referral Levels and Rewards

  • Level 1 Direct Referral: +5 Tap Tickets
  • Level 2 Referral: If your direct referral refers someone, they'll also earn Tap Tickets. However, the referral is only considered successful if the person you referred buys a booster!
  • Level 3 Referral: +3 Tap Tickets for you and your referral when a booster is purchased.
  • Level 4 Referral: +2 Tap Tickets for every successful referral who buys a booster.
  • Level 5 Referral: +1 Tap Ticket for every completed referral.

Important Note:

The referral system only works if the person you referred buys a booster. Simply referring them through links won't trigger the rewards.

Referral Multiplier:

Referring others also increases your multiplier chances! Currently, the max multiplier is between 2x and 3x, starting from a base multiplier of 1.4x. This multiplier can increase based on random percentages, depending on how many successful referrals you have.
